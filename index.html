The brief

A two-model steel business running on paper

A steel round-bar MSME in Gujarat buys and processes bars to sell, and also processes customers' material for a fee — with no single source of truth.

The problem

  • Orders, stock, job cards & finance on paper, spreadsheets, and WhatsApp.
  • Error-prone money & GST maths; risky sale-vs-job-work confusion.
  • No audit trail, no real-time view of stock, receivables, or production.
  • Off-the-shelf ERPs are costly and don't fit draw-bench + job-work reality.

The solution

  • One internal Android app for the entire order-to-cash chain.
  • Exact money (integer paise) + automated GST split, enforced by the backend.
  • Double-entry books that reconcile; append-only, tamper-evident ledgers.
  • Purpose-built, owned outright, ₹0/month to run.
The domain

Two business models, one system

The core product insight: keep Purchase–Sale and Job-Work strictly separate so a customer's material is never valued as company stock or claimed for GST input.

Purchase–Sale

Buy → draw → sell

Buy raw bars, process them on draw benches into finished bright bars, sell on a full GST invoice. Material enters stock value; GST input credit applies.

Job-Work

Process customer material

Customer's own material comes in, is processed, and returned — billed as charges only (SAC). It never enters the company's stock value and claims no GST input.

Sales Order→ Supplier PO / Inward→ Job Card (draw bench)→ Finished goods→ Dispatch + GST→ Payment

Every step posts a balanced double-entry voucher to the ledger.

How it's built

A free-first, self-hostable stack — with the correctness and security bar of a much bigger team.

Capacitor 6React 18TypeScript (strict) Vite 6SupabasePostgreSQL Row-Level SecurityEdge FunctionsTanStack Query ZustandZodVitestGitHub Actions
  • Double-entry finance with a reconciliation check (verify_ledger() balanced)
  • Append-only financial & stock ledgers (UPDATE/DELETE trigger-blocked)
  • Row-Level Security — finance data denied to the operations role
  • Integer-paise money & integer milli-kg quantity — never a float
  • Automated intra/inter-state GST split; job-work billed charges-only
